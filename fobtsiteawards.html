"FOBT Site Awards"


As from September we will be awarding one Bookmaker per Week our FOBT Best Site Award. Let us know which Bookmaker is worthy of this Award by emailing us with your reasons. Are the machines sited well, are they well kept? Do they have a stool for added comfort? Whatever your reason let us know and give your local bookie a pat on the back!

We will spot check the bookmakers shop that receives the most nominations and take a few pictures and even sample their FOBT wares, to see if they are as good as you say, and will give the most worthy our coveted Award

However if you wish to nominate a bookmaker for having the worst kept FOBT's checkout our Mr Sheen Awards
