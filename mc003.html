We receive on average 19 disputes per week, (however it has to be said the majority of these are for non OPA Approved casinos) with this in mind it is a noble achievement for a casino to have such a first class customer support and in-house management structure that they are able to rectify any problem a player may have experienced immediately and in such a way there was no need to seek our player dispute resolution service.

We are proud that so many of our Approved Casinos achieved this remarkable level of customer service, which is further proof that Online Players Association approved casinos really are the "cream of the crop"

To ensure fair play and keep the integrity of the award any disputes that turned out to be fraudulent ( yes we occasionally get a few of them) or those submitted with malicious intent do not count as a valid dispute.
