WowPot 5 Reel Video Slot



WowPot 5 Reel Video Slot is a five reel, fifteen payline, and fifteen coin slot machine. The multiple paylines increase your chances of winning. Note: In WowPot Video Slot you can only bet one coin per payline. Therefore, you can bet a maximum of 15 coins.

WowPot Video Slot has a wild, multiplier symbol, a scatter symbol, and a Progressive Jackpot.

The WowPot symbol is a wild, multiplier symbol. This means it substitutes for any other symbol to complete winning combinations, except the Scatter symbol. The WowPot symbol doubles the payout of any combination it completes.

Multiple WowPot symbols on an enabled payline create WowPot winning combinations. WowPot winning combination payouts are not doubled. The WowPot symbol only multiplies payouts if it acts as a wild symbol.

The Scatter symbol does not need to appear in a line on an enabled payline to win. It can be scattered anywhere on the five reels, provided three or more Scatter symbols appear.

You only qualify for the Progressive Jackpot if you bet the maximum of fifteen coins per spin. This is one coin per payline. If you are playing the Progressive, and five WowPot symbols line up on the fifteenth enabled payline, you win the Progressive Jackpot.
