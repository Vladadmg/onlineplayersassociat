    Why should a player join the OPA?

    It is unfortunate but true that the international online gambling industry is largely unregulated, and that means two things:

    The player needs to do his or her homework to avoid involvement with a rogue casino and certain types of lower quality casino operators can and do indulge in bad casino practise to the player's disadvantage.

    The Online Players' Association gives players the strength of unity and community, with free membership
    • a private message board that enables the exchange of vital information with other honest and experienced gamblers, and industry specialists.
    • an informative newsletter based on input from OPA researchers and other volunteer experts serving on the OPA Players' Advisory Board.
    • unbiased and expertly assembled lists of "Approved" and "Not Recommended" operations
    • a free, influential dispute resolution service that has already recovered many hundreds of thousands of dollars for cheated players. Someone to fight your corner with skill and determination when you have a genuine case.
    Why should a casino join the OPA?

    Honest and efficient online casinos have everything to gain from joining the Online Players' Association as a casino member, primarily because it brings the operation into close and frequent proximity with a significant pool of regular online gamblers of integrity who have subscribed to the Association's Code of Ethics and Conduct.

    One of the OPA's major goals is to bring honest, genuine online gamblers into contact with casinos of quality and integrity.

    OPA Casino Members apply here free of charge and must agree to the Association's Casino Code of Conduct and Ethics.

    The applicant's track record and the content of the website and gambling software is independently tested and assessed by a panel of experienced gamblers and industry specialists providing a voluntary service to the Association.

    Casinos that do not make the grade are informed why. Those that do are immediately entitled to carry the OPA's "Approved Casino" logo, are listed on the busy OPA Forum as "Approved OPA Casinos" and receive publicity on the CasinoGazette and Casinomeister webcast shows. Advertising and promotional possibilities can be discussed if desired.

    Why should a portal support us?

    Gambling portals of integrity are welcomed free as Online Players' Association Supporters, entitled to carry the "OPA Supporter" logo that tells players that the operation has been independently vetted and is felt to offer an acceptable level of sound gambling information.

    The operators have member's access to the valuable facilities of the OPA listed above. In this way portal operators can keep abreast of industry and casino issues being handled by the Association, assisting judgement calls concerning affiliate and advertising contracts.
