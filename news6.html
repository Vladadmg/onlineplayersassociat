FOBT News

Satellite Information Services (SIS) and Cyberview Technology (Cyberview) today announced a distribution agreement, whereby SIS will distribute Cyberview’s Fixed Odds Betting Terminals (FOBTs) to the UK independent bookmaker market.

The ABB announcement of the Code of Conduct brings certainty to suppliers and bookmakers alike as to the future of FOBTs. Confidence in the market and the full compliance of Cyberview’s system and content with the Code, make now the ideal time for SIS to distribute these powerful solutions.

SIS identified that Cyberview’s FOBTs are of the highest standard available in the industry, offering unrivalled levels of security and reliability, coupled with superior reporting and auditing capability. SIS provides live television, audio and data services to almost every betting outlet in the UK and Ireland. By working with Cyberview SIS are able to add value to this service by supplying their customers with the leading FOBT on the market.

Chairman, Jean-Marie Gatto said, “We are delighted to be working with SIS, and believe their knowledge of the industry, their existing relationships with over 9,000 LBOs and their extensive experience delivering high tech solutions perfectly compliments the technological and operational benefits of Cyberview’s FOBTs.”

David Holdgate, Chief Executive of SIS said “In selecting Cyberview as our partner in the FOBT market, we have undoubtedly chosen the market leader who delivers not only the best technological solution, with unparalleled security and audit ability, but also the best value for our client bookmakers.” To date, Roulette has proved to be by far the most popular betting opportunity available on Cyberview’s FOBT. However, to ensure users continue to enjoy a varied and exciting betting experience, Cyberview are currently developing new and original content to bring added appeal to FOBT users.

Cyberview Technology is the leading supplier of FOBTs within the UK. In addition, the company has activities in the North America, Czech Republic, Russia, Asia, Caribbean and South America, and has become a world leader in the technology of downloadable, server-based systems within a variety of betting and gaming environments.

SIS offers over 16 years of unrivalled industry knowledge and expertise to bookmakers. In addition to live coverage of horse and dog racing, plus worldwide distribution of betting information, SIS provide a comprehensive range of services to the betting industry. These include iSIS, the most advanced display solution available, together with Bet Edit and Sports Data information feeds. This wide range of services provides bookmakers with high levels of control and flexibility over their businesses.
