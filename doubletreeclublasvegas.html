Doubletree Club Hotel

The non-gaming Doubletree Club Hotel Las Vegas Airport is conveniently located two miles south of McCarran Airport, and two miles east of I-15 at I-215 (Airport Connector) and Warm Springs Road (Exit 7). We provide complimentary 24-hour McCarran Airport shuttle service, along with scheduled shuttle service to and from the MGM Grand and local businesses within 1.5 miles. Our casual, friendly hotel, situated just minutes southeast of the Las Vegas Strip, offers comfort, value and service to business and leisure travelers alike.

Our tastefully appointed guest rooms and executive accommodations feature ample work desks, decadently comfortable Sweet Dreams beds, and wireless high-speed internet access. For your convenience, complementary wireless HSIA is available in lobby level public areas. Recreational amenities include a sparkling, heated outdoor pool and a complimentary 24-hour Fitness Center for cardio workouts.

Our unique Doubletree Club Room -- equal parts office, den and café -- features an Au Bon Pain bakery café, lounge, and 24-hour self-serve business center with computer, HSIA, laptop connection and remote access printer. There are also complimentary mini meeting rooms and personal work spaces, as well as comfortable conversation areas to relax with friends or business associates.

With flexible and functional event space, the Doubletree Club hotel is an ideal choice for gatherings of eight to 80 people. For conferences, business meetings or social occasions, we supply on-site audio visual rentals, wired high-speed internet access, and full-service catering. Our attentive, friendly staff will ensure flawless events.

When it's time to relax, you'll find golf, fabulous shopping and dining, and everything that Las Vegas is famous for just moments away. Try your luck on the Strip or experience world-famous shows and performers. Whether you're in Las Vegas for business, vacation, or weekend getaway, our caring, helpful staff looks forward to welcoming you with our special, signature warm chocolate chip cookie at check-in.
