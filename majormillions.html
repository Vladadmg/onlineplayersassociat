Major Millions

Major Millions is a three reel, three payline, and three coin slot machine. The multiple paylines increase your chances of winning.

Major Millions has a wild, multiplier symbol and a Progressive Jackpot. These features are activated when you bet three coins.

The Major Millions symbol is wild and substitutes for any other symbol to complete winning combinations. A single Major Millions symbol doubles the payout of any combination it completes. Two Major Millions symbols quadruple the payout of any combination they complete.
