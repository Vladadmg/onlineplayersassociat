Big Top

Big Top is a five reel, nine payline, and nine coin slot machine. The multiple paylines increase your chances of winning.

Big Top has a wild symbol, and a scatter symbol.

The Wild Clown symbol is wild. This means that it substitutes for any other symbol to complete a winning combination, except the Scatter Monkey symbol.

The Scatter Monkey symbol is a scatter symbol. This means that it does not need to appear in a line on an enabled payline to win. It can be scattered anywhere on the five reels, provided three or more Scatter Monkey symbols appear.
