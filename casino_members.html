    Welcome to The Online Players' Associations Approved Casino section.

    Once a casino has applied to join, the casino is then checked over by our volunteer Advisory Board made up of experienced online players and gaming consultants who report back their findings to The Executive Committee. The Executive Committee reviews the information gathered and decide whether or not to allow the casino to become OPA Approved. Casinos must be in operation for 6 months in order to join. This is waived only when the operation is a sister casino of a member that is in good standing. If a casino doesn't pass on merit they are invited to re-apply in 6 months and during this time we will continue to monitor their operation.
