Keno

Keno uses a large board marked with the numbers 1 to 80.

In a basket are balls also numbered 1 to 80. Twenty balls are drawn at random.

The objective is to predict the numbers of the twenty balls drawn. You may choose to bet on up to fifteen of the eighty numbers on the board in one play.
